## 2. Minimum detectable effect (paired McNemar, alpha=.05, power=.80)

          optimistic (e=.05)  conservative (e=.10)
  n=25                 39 pp                 46 pp
  n=30                 34 pp                 40 pp
  -> Under ANY plausible noise assumption the MDE is ~34-46pp. Reframe the null as 'no
     tool effect larger than ~the MDE,' NOT 'no effect.' A commercially-decisive 5-10pp
     lift is far below our MDE -> structurally invisible to this design.
